PREAMBLE :
The Government of Maharashtra Delegation included Shri. P.Velrasu, IAS,
CEO, (MIDC) and Shri. Kunal Kemnar, Joint CEO (MIDC) to visit "Tokyo Big
Sight ', Japan between 17th December to 20th December, 2025 (including travel time)
to attend "SEMICON JAPAN, 2025" roundtable conference.
2. Since this programme was organised to bring together many important Global
Semiconductor Stakeholders at one location, Government of Maharashtra attended
this programme to boost the growth of our semiconductor sector and to strengthen
cooperation between India and Japan to advance the development of a robust
semiconductor ecosystem in India.

3. All the members of the official delegation will be entitled for travel expenses
and per diem allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
4. State Government is pleased to authorize the absence of Shri. P.Velrasu, IAS,
CEO,(MIDC) and Shri. Kunal Khemnar, Joint CEO (MIDC) from 17th December to
20th December, 2025 (including travel time) and be treated as on duty until they
resume their duties on return to India from their foreign tour.
5. State Government is further pleased to approve the ex-post facto sanction for
processing fees of visa for Japan, as per actual and medical facilities admissible, under
the revised AMA Scheme continued in the Ministry of External Affairs
O.M.No.Q/GA/653/1/74 dated 5/3/1979 for the members of the delegation. The
Government is also pleased to approve the ex-post facto sanction charges for medical
insurance for them.
6. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation etc. for the members of
the delegation will be borned by Maharashtra Industrial development Corporation
(MIDC).
7. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions
if any should be submitted to the government within one month from the date of
returning.
