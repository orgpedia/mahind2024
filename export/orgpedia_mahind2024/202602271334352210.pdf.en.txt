The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(1) The Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (FRBM) Act is required to ensure long-term economic viability by controlling the fiscal deficit, managing debt, increasing cost transparency, and setting fiscal targets. The government guarantee for raising debt for the corporation is being given on the condition that it will be necessary to comply with the provisions of this Act.
(2) What measures have been taken by the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation for the repayment of the proposed liability. The terms and conditions of how these loans will be repaid in case of debt exhaustion, the amount of interest that will be charged in case of delay in repayment of the loan, the period in which this penal interest will be repaid, etc. need to be clearly stated in the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to be made with the financial institution. The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation shall be required to submit regular (monthly or half-yearly) reports to the Government on repayment of loans and financial position.
(3) The expenditure should be made on the same matters for which the loan is to be raised. Also, the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation will be fully responsible for completing the works under this within the stipulated time.
(4) The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) should provide clarity as to whether or not such a loan can be raised if it is available at a lower interest rate from another institution for the same purpose during the period of borrowing from the financial institution, and also as to whether or not a one-time settlement facility can be provided. It should also examine what benefits a one-time settlement will bring to the corporation.
