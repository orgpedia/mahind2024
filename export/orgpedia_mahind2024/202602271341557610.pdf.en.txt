Introduction: - Competitive bidding process based on power tariff for setting up transmission plants in the state.
The government has decided to implement Tariff Based Competitive Bidding (808). An Empowered Committee has been constituted under 04.01.2019.
The 9th meeting of the committee was held on. Done on 12.01.2026. The meeting discussed the appointment of Bid Process Coordinator for 07 projects.
Accordingly, it was under Jhasana's consideration to appoint a tender process coordinator for 07 projects.
Government Decision: - Competitive tender based on power tariff for setting up new transmission plants in the state.
In the 9th meeting of the Empowered Committee for Implementation of the process, the following 07 projects were mentioned for implementation 1) M / s PFC Consulting Ltd. (PFCCL) and 2) M / s REC Power Developer & Consulting Ltd. The appointment of (RECPDCL) as Bid Process Coordinator (BPC) is being approved by the Government.
