PREAMBLE
Representatives from the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC)
have been invited by Datwyler Holding AG and its subsidiary, Datwyler Pharma Packaging India Pvt Ltd, to visit their global headquarters in Altdorf, Switzerland, with the aim of
promoting industrial growth in the state. Additionally, the Indo-German Chamber of
Commerce in Germany has invited the MIDC delegation for meetings with potential
investors in the state.
2. The meeting in Switzerland will discuss the proposed expansion of Datwyler Pharma
Packaging India Pvt Ltd in the Satara industrial area. It will also discuss industrial
development policy, infrastructure, planning and investment facilitation. Similarly, during the
visit to Germany, investment and technology exchange opportunities will be available
between the industrial ecosystem of the state and the German advanced manufacturing and innovation sector.
3. Believing that participation in this program will help attract investment to Maharashtra and encourage young entrepreneurs in the state to establish businesses, the Government of Maharashtra has accepted the invitation from Datwyler Holding AG in
Switzerland and the Indo-German Chamber of Commerce in Germany, and with the approval
of the Chief Minister of Maharashtra, has decided to send a state-level delegation to the
meetings in Switzerland and Germany, which will take place from January 17, 2026 to
December 24, 2026 (including travel time).
RESOLUTION:-
The Government of Maharashtra is hereby granting ex-post facto approval for the visit of a state-level delegation to Switzerland and Germany from 17" January,2026 to 24"
January, 2026 (including travel time), to attend meetings organized by Datwyler Holding AG
and Indo-German Chamber of Commerce.
